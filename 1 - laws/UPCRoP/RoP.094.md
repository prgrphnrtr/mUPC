## Rule 94 - Invitation to the President of the European Patent Office  to comment

The judge-rapporteur may, on his own initiative or on request by the President of the European Patent Office, invite the President of the European Patent Office to comment in writing on any question arising in the course of proceedings under this Section  including any appeal by the claimant pursuant to Rule 220.
